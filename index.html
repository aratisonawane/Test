Transparent Charity System Using Blockchain Technology



Awesome Features

The blockchain service layer implements the functions of distributed accounting of the charity platform, including package block, get consensus on transaction, broadcast block, and synchronize data to a local database. The storage layer is used to store data, including blockchain storage and local storage

Increases Trust

The easiest way blockchain creates the trust is via providing proof of ownership. When you have a blockchain-based asset, you can be sure that no one can take it away from you without your permission. It is because each transaction is recorded on the blockchain, and everyone has a copy of the ledger

Security

Your data is sensitive and crucial, and blockchain can significantly change how your critical information is viewed. By creating a record that can’t be altered and is encrypted end-to-end, blockchain helps prevent fraud and unauthorized activity

Transparency

Without blockchain, each organization has to keep a separate database. Because blockchain uses a distributed ledger, transactions and data are recorded identically in multiple locations

Traceability of data shared across a business network

Blockchain creates an audit trail that documents the provenance of an asset at every step on its journey. In industries where consumers are concerned about environmental or human rights issues surrounding a product — or an industry troubled by counterfeiting and fraud — this helps provide the proof.

The Bebefits of Blockchain Technology


Blockchain eliminates the need for trusted intermediaries, and hence the transaction can be executed at a higher speed. The Blockchain build trust with contributors, beneficiaries, and different partners to contact the ideal individuals and improve organization expenses and adequacy.

Because of eliminating the need for intermediaries, each transaction's cost gets reduced in the Blockchain Environment. Besides, Blockchain brings down regulatory costs; more assets can arrive at the right, needy recipient.

Transparency is compulsory for this situation. Blockchain Technology works on Trustless Theme, and hence ultimately increases the clarity of the transaction.

The primary pain of high transaction costs is eliminated, which allows the Donor to transfer more money to the recipient at minimal or no charge.

It's challenging to trace where the money is going in the Blockchain Technology Era. Higher the audit trail, lower the uncertainty, and cyber-crime from the philanthropy world.

Everybody can make a donor account and utilize the platform to help the causes they wish to. The critical aspect is that donors will have all access to the charity's immutable records of payment amounts and routes. Moreover, a donor account can be private if the Donor wishes to stay unknown.
